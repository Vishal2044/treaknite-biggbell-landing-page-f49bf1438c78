Frequently Asked Questions

What is BiggBell?

BiggBell is an innovative platform that connects brands with influencers for powerful marketing campaigns. It provides tools to streamline the process of finding, managing, and analyzing influencer collaborations.

How does BiggBell work?

BiggBell allows brands to create campaigns, find suitable influencers, and track campaign performance. Influencers can sign up, showcase their profiles, and connect with brands to collaborate on projects.

Is there a cost to use BiggBell?

A part of Biggbell platform is free to use BiggBell and it will be. Biggbell also offers various subscription plans based on the needs of brands and influencers. You can choose a plan that suits your requirements or contact us for custom solutions.

How do I get started on BiggBell?

Simply sign up on the platform, create your profile, and start exploring! Brands can post campaigns and influencers can respond to relevant opportunities.

How secure is my data on BiggBell?

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
